 The Laxmi Group Companies

Lexis International Limited (LIL)

Established in 2012 as the core construction arm of the Laxmi Group, Lexis International Limited serves the East African market with operations in Kenya, Uganda, and Rwanda. The company partners with governments, multinationals, and various institutions to manage their building and civil engineering projects. Collaborating with Eacon Contracting Limited, a Laxmi Group subsidiary, Lexis International Limited is a regional leader in Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) projects in the Oil & Gas, Healthcare, and Data Centre sectors.

Learn More 

Eacon Contracting Limited (ECL)

Founded in 2015, Eacon Contracting Limited (ECL) offers corporate leadership, governance, and professional construction management expertise within the Laxmi Group. ECL aligns the Group’s operations with global best practices in Project Management, Engineering Design Management, Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE), and Quality Control.As a General Contractor or through a strategic partnership with Laxmi Group subsidiaries, ECL has successfully delivered projects in the Healthcare, Interior Fit-out, Telecommunications, Data Centres, and Oil & Gas sectors in the region.

Learn More 

Gokul Builders Limited (GBL)

Gokul Builders Limited, the pioneer company within Laxmi Group, was incorporated in 2011. The company focuses on offering construction services for residential, institutional, and commercial projectsto private developers and government institutions.The company is recognized for its leadership in collaborating with real estate development companiesto create sustainable and affordable housing solutions in the region.

Our ​mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional construction services and built spaces that meet our clients' conceptual requirements through innovative solutions. We prioritize our customers' needs and ensure we build resilient, high-quality, functional, and sustainable spaces for the present and future.

Our commitment to excellence in the built environment industry is guided by our core values of integrity, sustainability, and safe systems of work, creating lasting value and positive impacts in the communities we serve.

Our experienced engineering and management personnel empower us to match and exceed global best practices in Project Management, Engineering Design, Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE), Value Engineering, and Quality Control while continuously integrating innovative approaches.

Laxmi Group continuously invests in process improvement with every project executed and proactively nurtures our talented people to strive and ensure our solutions and services exceed our client's expectations and best serve our community.

Our ​vision

The Company’s vision of growth is to be one of the leading construction and consultancy service providers in the country. 

Our Values

our values are the foundation upon which we build every project and relationship. They are our pillars of strength, guiding our journey toward excellence and innovation.

Quality

Quality is central to all our group activities and is assured in every discipline and stage of the project. We view quality from two perspectives: the actual production of quality work and the perception by project stakeholders, both internal and external, that a quality product or service has been delivered. Both substance and perception hold equal value. Our commitment to quality is passionate and continuous.

Client First

We anticipate our clients' needs and take targeted actions to satisfy their ultimate goals, always operating within our own values. A crucial aspect of our customer orientation is dependability, ensuring that products, schedules, appearances, and clarity are consistently maintained. We are committed to always delivering on our commitments.

Innovation

To be leaders in our respective professions, we must maintain a never-ending quest for new ideas. This pursuit encompasses the adoption of new technological advancements, the development of homegrown innovations, and the application of concepts developed elsewhere. These new ideas include technical process innovations and strategies designed to control costs efficiently, all while upholding our quality objectives as the ultimate goal.

Integrity

We recognise that challenges will arise as we strive to satisfy our clients’ needs. Progress is made through honesty with ourselves and each other. All our engagements, both internal and external, are conducted in an honest and transparent manner. We strongly believe that leaders' effectiveness and efficiency stem not from their position but from the respect earned through their performance.

Passion

We undertake all our work with a profound sense of passion, recognizing that enthusiasm is integral to maintaining the meticulous attention to detail that distinguishes quality work from mediocrity. Our management philosophy is founded upon responsibility and mutual respect. Laxmi Group upholds an environment and structure that fosters productivity and respect for both customers and employees alike.
