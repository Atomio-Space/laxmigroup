Front-End Engineering and Design (FEED)

Our team's professional training and combined industry experience in Front-End Engineering and Design (FEED) enable us to provide our clients with an astute and detailed project plan with all the project's technical and economic data to proceed with detailed engineering design and construction.

Get a Quote 

Key Benefits

Technical Planning

Define equipment specs, P&IDs, and material requirements

Budget Precision

Deliver estimates with ±10% accuracy through risk-rated costing

Risk-Managed Execution

Systematic approach to project risks and mitigation

key Features

 ​​​Preliminary Engineering Design

Complete design documentation for major project components, including detailed drawings and outline specifications tailored to your project requirements.

 ​​​Detailed Cost Estimation

Detailed cost summaries for all major project components, including contingencies, ensuring transparent and accurate financial planning.

 ​​​Project Schedule Development

Comprehensive project timeline using Gantt charts and Critical Path Method (CPM) to outline tasks, durations, dependencies, and resource allocation.

 ​​​Risk Management Planning

Thorough risk assessment and mitigation strategies to identify and address potential project challenges proactively

 ​​​Procurement Planning

Strategic procurement planning including lead times, supplier evaluation, and development of procurement packages (EOIS and RFPs).

 ​​Regulatory Compliance

Complete handling of statutory approvals, environmental impact assessments, and necessary project development permits.

Benefits

  • Reduced project uncertainty and enhanced decision-making capability 
  • Optimized cost control through detailed planning and estimation 
  • Improved project efficiency through comprehensive scheduling 
  • Enhanced stakeholder alignment and communication 
  • Minimized regulatory compliance risks 
  • Streamlined transition to detailed engineering phase
