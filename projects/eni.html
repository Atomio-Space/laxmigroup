Proposed Construction Works of Makueni Agrihub Facility for ENI Kenya B.V

Project Overview

This project entails the development of multiple facilities for the Makueni Agrihub, including process line buildings, a workshop, and a warehouse. Additional infrastructure includes a powerhouse, firefighting pump room, bale storage shed, underground storage tanks, and oil storage tanks. The scope also covers extensive MEP installations and the construction of boundary walls.

Client 
ENI Kenya B.V 
Architect 
Brickehaus 
Location 
Makueni, Kenya 
Timeline 
39 Weeks

Scope of Work

  • Construction of process line buildings and a workshop
  • Development of a warehouse and powerhouse
  • Bale storage shed installation
  • Supporting infrastructure, including boundary walls and drainage systems
  • MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) installations
