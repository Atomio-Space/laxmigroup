Proposed​ Safaricom Limuru Data Center Project

Client 
Safaricom PLC
Architect 
AKA Studio Limited 
Location 
Limuru, Kenya 
Timeline 
40 Weeks

Project Overview

This state-of-the-art data center project encompasses the construction of multiple specialized facilities designed to meet Safaricom's expanding digital infrastructure needs. The project demonstrates our expertise in handling complex technical requirements while maintaining the highest standards of construction quality.

Scope of Work

  • Construction of a two-storey data centre with advanced cooling and security systems
  • Development of 2 power centers ensuring redundant power supply
  • Installation of specialized pump room and 5 gatehouses for enhanced security
  • Complete interior fit-outs and furnishing
  • Comprehensive infrastructure including roads, boundary walls, and drainage systems
